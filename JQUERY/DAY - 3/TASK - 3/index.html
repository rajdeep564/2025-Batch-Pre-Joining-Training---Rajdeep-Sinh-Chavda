Tax Slab:

For Men:

Income: up to 2.4L - No Tax

Income: 2.4L to 6L - 10%

For Women:

Income: up to 2.6L - No Tax

Income: 2.6L to 7L - 10%

Income: above 7L - 20%

For Senior Citizen:

Income: up to 3L - No Tax

Income: 3L to 7L - 10%

Income: above 7L - 20%

Tax Exemptions:

1. 80% of the Loan amount or 20% of total income, whichever is less, is non-taxable.

Example: If your Total Income is 200 and the Loan is 100, then 80% of the Loan = 80 INR and 20% of Income = 40 INR. The lesser amount (40 INR) is non-taxable, and 160 INR is taxable.

2. Any amount invested up to 1,00,000 INR (1L) is non-taxable.

Example: If you invested 1,60,000 INR, then 1,00,000 INR is the non-taxable amount.
